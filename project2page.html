University of Jeddah Student Reference (UJSR)

A centralized portal to enhance access to academic resources and services for students at the University of Jeddah.

Project Overview

The UJSR portal addresses challenges that students face in accessing essential academic resources and services. It provides a secure and user-friendly interface integrated with the universityâ€™s database to protect user data and simplify access to information.

Main Features

  • Login: Secure access for students and coordinators using University ID and password.
  • Home Page: Displays site services, including user profile, registered courses, and GPA calculator.
  • User Page: Displays academic information like ID, college, specialization, and grades.
  • Course Materials: Access to course materials such as books, slides, and assignments.
  • Course Explanations: Video explanations uploaded by coordinators, with student feedback options.
  • GPA Calculation: Allows students to calculate their GPA based on their grades.
  • University E-services: Quick access to administrative and technical services at the university.

Stakeholders

  • IT Center: Manages system operations and network.
  • Colleges: Oversee course content.
  • Coordinators: Responsible for modifying and uploading course materials.
  • Students: Main users who access resources and services.

Requirements

Functional Requirements

  • Students can view and download course materials.
  • Coordinators can upload and manage content.
  • Students can calculate their GPA.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • Security: Secure handling of user credentials and data transmission.
  • Accessibility: 24/7 availability with a quick recovery in case of failure.
  • Efficiency: Fast page load times for a smooth user experience.
